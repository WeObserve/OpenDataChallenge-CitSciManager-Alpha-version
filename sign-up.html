Looking to develop or host your citizen science projects?

Let us know your projects and requirements. We believe in open-source technologies and we will try to assemble a crew of technology avengers to help you develop tools in our fight against Climate change.

Our platform is currently in alpha development phase and we are working with 2-4 projects over the summer (July-Sept 2020) to help design, test and build a stable release. Expected launch is September 2020!

Feel free to fork our code ethically. Always check the correct licensing terms!
